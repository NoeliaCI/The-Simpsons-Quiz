The Simpsons Quiz Game

How To Play

This quiz will test your knowdlege on The Simpsons Series.

Answer the question by selecting one of the 4 answer choices.

If you get 5 wrong answers your game will be over.

You need to get 10 right answers to unlock the next level.

At the end of the game you can save your score and check your place in the leaderboard.

Good luck and have fun!
